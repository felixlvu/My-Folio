My-Folio

The aim of this personal project is to showcase my projects and experience in IT, and more specifically in the field of coding.
I wanted to create this portfolio in the form of a website to familiarize myself with the "front-end" using the "HTML", "CSS" and "JS" languages.

This project has proved to be very instructive so far, particularly with the addition of my API, which has enabled me to understand how sites work and the difference between "back-end" and "front-end".

Thanks to this project, I've realized how important it is to have a solid plan before starting a project, because without a plan, you run the risk of starting the work over again and again, and getting sidetracked in every direction. After starting out in the Web field, I realized that this was an aspect of IT that interested me enormously.
I haven't yet made a decision about my professional future, but it could be a possibility to consider.
